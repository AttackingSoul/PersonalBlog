---
title: 'How to trim video with ffmpeg'
publishedAt: '2025-07-16'
summary: ''
---

```bash
ffmpeg -i input.mp4 -ss 00:01:30 -to 00:02:30 -c:v copy -c:a copy output.mp4
```

Parameter description:

-ss 00:01:30: start time (1 minute and 30 seconds)

-to 00:02:30: end time (2 minutes and 30 seconds)

-c:v copy -c:a copy: directly copy the audio and video stream (lossless and fast)
